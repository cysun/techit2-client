Welcome to TWR!

TechOps Work Requests (TWR) is a website that allows the lab coordinators to submit work requests (a.k.a. tickets) and keep track of the status of the requests.

Once a ticket is submitted, the supervisor of TechOps will be notified, and he or she will assign one or more lab technicians to work on the request. After the work is completed, the technician(s) will inform the supervisor, and the supervisor will review the work and update the status of the request accordingly.

Whenever the status of a request changes (including when an update is added to the ticket by a technician), all parties involved will be notified by email. You can also log onto the website to see the tickets you submitted, and the tickets you are in charge of if you are a supervisor or technician.
